Do you do anesthesia?

I work without anesthesia but not without exceptions. The effect of anesthesia manifests itself individually for each person. As practise shows it ia better to get a grip and to be a little patient for a good result.

Do you do B&W tattoos?

I also work with black pigment but I prefer color.

Deposit

You need to pay a deposit of 20% of the nogotiated price to book a session or book a sketch. The deposit is included in the full price of the session. The deposit is non-refundable if you cancel the session completely or reschedule it less than 72 hours before the scheduled date.

How is it possible to damage the tattoo?

Not following the tattoo master`s instructions for healing (yes, it is the most common cause of poor healing) Neglect to apply sunscreen in the summer. It should be applied not only before going to the beach but also before going otside when it is sunny weather. You should contact the master at the first manifestations of a questionable reaction of the skin to the tattoo.
